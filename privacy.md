Privacy Policy for LinkMeIn

LinkMeIn is a personal content planning and publishing tool for LinkedIn.

The application may access basic LinkedIn profile information, such as name, profile identifier, and email address, only for authentication and account connection purposes.

The application may request permission to publish posts to my LinkedIn profile after explicit approval.

LinkMeIn does not read LinkedIn feeds, contacts, connections, messages, likes, comments, or private data.

LinkMeIn does not scrape LinkedIn, automate browser behavior, auto-like, auto-comment, auto-connect, or send automated messages.

Any draft posts, images, schedules, and publishing data are used only for content planning and publishing through the application.

Access tokens, when used in a future backend version, will be stored securely and used only to publish posts that have been explicitly approved.

No personal data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.
